Detailed Description

Implementation of a directed graph.

Author
Ravi Gaddipati (rgadd.nosp@m.ip1@.nosp@m.jhu.e.nosp@m.du)
Date
June 26, 2016

Each node stores a sequence and relevant information. Graphs can be derived from other graphs with a filter, allowing the extraction of population subsets.
